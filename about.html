About Our Quiz

In today's world, we believe that too few people understand the impact of their diet on their overall health. Our quiz aims to educate individuals about the different foods they eat daily, highlighting both the positives and negatives. This quiz is not only a fun way to gather insights but also a means to teach others about balanced dietary choices.
